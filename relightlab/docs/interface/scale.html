Scale

You can specify a segment on the image and assign a length in millimiters. This will be used for taking measurements in the viewer and displaying the scale, once the RTI has been generated.

Click the 'Set new scale measurement...' button and click on the edges of the segment you know the length of, then enter the measurement in mm.

Pixel size and image with and height will be computed automatically.
