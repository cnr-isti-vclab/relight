
TFW format:

Pixel Size (X): The width of a pixel in map units (e.g., meters).
Rotation (Y): Rotation around the Y-axis (often 0). Our dataset has no rotation.
Rotation (X): Rotation around the X-axis (often 0). Our dataset has no rotation.
Pixel Size (Y): The height of a pixel in map units (usually negative).
X-Coordinate: The X-coordinate of the center of the top-left pixel.
Y-Coordinate: The Y-coordinate of the center of the top-left pixel.
